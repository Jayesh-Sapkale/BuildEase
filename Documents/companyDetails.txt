#######################################################################################################

** Column names and description

1. company_id: Unique identifier for the company.

2. name: Name of the company.

3. address:
	street: Street address.
	city: City.
	state: State/Province.
	zip_code: ZIP/Postal code.
	country: Country.

4. contact_details:
	phone_number: Contact phone number.
	email: Contact email address.
	website: Company website URL.

5. registration_number: Official registration number.

6. license_number: License number for building and construction.

7. company_profile: Brief description of the company.

8. key_personnel: List of key personnel (e.g., CEO, project manager).
	name: Name of the person.

9. services_offered: List of services the company offers (e.g., residential building, commercial building, renovation).

10. awards: List of awards the company has received.
	award_name: Name of the award.
	award_year: Year the award was received.

11. customer_reviews: Reviews and ratings from customers.
	customer_id: Unique identifier for the customer.
	rating: Rating given by the customer (e.g., out of 5).
	review: Written review by the customer.
	review_date: Date the review was submitted.

12. partners: List of partner companies or organizations.
	partner_name: Name of the partner.
	partner_type: Type of partner (e.g., supplier, subcontractor).
